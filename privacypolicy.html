Privacy Policy

How We Handle and Protect Your Information

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, address, and service details when you book an electrician or fill out our contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to confirm bookings, provide services, send updates, and improve your experience. Your data helps us deliver faster and better support.

4. Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to secure your information against unauthorized access or misuse. Only authorized team members can access customer data.

5. Sharing Information

We never sell or rent your data. We may share it with our verified electricians or service partners only when necessary to complete your request.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if preferred.

7. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through our support email or phone number.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us through our website, email, or customer helpline.
